Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension helps you fill in Salesforce cases using templates. We respect your privacy.

What We Collect

  • No personal data (names, emails, etc.).
  • Website domain (e.g. companyname.my.salesforce.com) for usage counting.

How We Use Data

We only use the website domain to count how many cases you fill in per company.

Where Data Is Stored

All templates and case details stay on your device. We do not send them anywhere.

Usage Statistics

We send only the website domain (no user or case details) to our server to track how often the extension is used per company.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.
